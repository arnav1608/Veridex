About Veridex

Transparency through structured discussion

What is Veridex?

Veridex is a claim verification and discussion platform that enables users to critically evaluate information through community-driven analysis. Each claim has its own discussion space to encourage structured debate.

Problem Statement

Misinformation spreads rapidly across digital platforms, often without accountability or context. Users lack tools to evaluate claims objectively.

Our Approach

  • Claim-specific discussion boards
  • Multiple content formats
  • Authenticated participation
  • Community voting and replies
  • Discussion maturity indicators

Technology Stack

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Backend: Node.js, Express
  • Database: MongoDB
